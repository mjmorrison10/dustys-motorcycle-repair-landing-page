# Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair – Project Summary

## Overview
A complete strategic overhaul of the landing page for Dusty's Motorcycle Service & Repair (Orange, CA). The project transformed a basic site into a high-converting, locally optimized platform designed to capture leads automatically when the owner is unable to answer the phone.

## Completed Phases

### Phase 1: Local Market & Business Intelligence
- Identified high-intent keywords and competitive gaps
- Defined unique positioning: “The honest neighborhood mechanic at the gateway to every ride”

### Phase 2: Digital Asset Audit
- Audited Instagram, Yelp, Facebook, Google Maps, and Nextdoor
- Extracted real customer language and the approved circular logo

### Phase 3: Codebase Review
- Full technical audit of the existing site
- Identified critical gaps in lead capture, SEO, and mobile experience

### Phase 4: High-Conversion Copywriting
- Complete rewrite of all major sections
- Shifted CTAs to “Text or Book Online”
- Added LocalBusiness schema

### Phase 5: Refactoring Roadmap
- Delivered prioritized execution plan

### Phase 6: Implementation
- Integrated approved logo
- Added sticky mobile CTA bar
- Added Google Maps embed + directions
- Added Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags
